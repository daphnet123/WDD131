Crochet Projects

Here are some of the baby blankets I've made! If you want to make this, here's what you'll need:

10mm Crochet hook

2 skeins of Bernat Baby Blanket yarn

When you're stitching, I recommend to chain 75-100, depending on how wide you want the blanket.

Then Double Crochet the rest of the time until you like the length of the blanket.

Here are some blankets I have been working on. These typically take longer because they are a lot larger in size compared to a baby blanket.

The types of yarn I use vary depending on the texture I want. Same with hook size and stitch type, but I prefer Double Crochet stitches overall.
